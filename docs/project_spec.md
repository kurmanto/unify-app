## Overview
Unify App is a comprehensive practice management application for **Unify Rolfing** (unifyrolfing.com), a solo Rolfing Structural Integration practice in Toronto, Canada.

## Goals
- Replace fragmented tools with a single, purpose-built system
- Handle full clinical and business workflow: booking, payments, client management, SOAP notes, Ten Series tracking, intake forms, marketing campaigns, and business analytics
- PIPEDA/PHIPA compliant for Canadian healthcare data
- Mobile-first responsive design for practitioner use on phone
- Embeddable booking widget for the practice website

## Users
- **Practitioner (Tomer):** Manages all aspects of the practice
- **Clients:** Book sessions via embeddable widget, fill intake forms
- **Future:** Other bodywork practitioners (multi-tenant SaaS)

## Scope
The initial release covers:
1. Authentication and practitioner dashboard
2. Client management with health history
3. Appointment booking and scheduling
4. Calendar views (day/week)
5. Payment processing (Stripe + Square)
6. SOAP notes with AI-powered voice dictation
7. Ten Series protocol engine
8. Digital intake forms with e-signatures
9. Email marketing campaigns
10. Business analytics dashboard
11. Embeddable booking widget
